# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 2025

This Chrome extension (“the Extension”) respects your privacy. The Extension is designed to operate entirely on your device and does **not** send any collected data to external servers.

## Information the Extension Collects

The Extension collects only the information **you explicitly choose to provide**, such as:

* Website data or page content you select.
* Any text, images, or metadata you intentionally feed into the Extension.

This data is used **solely for generating the results or features provided by the Extension**, and remains on your device.

## How the Data Is Processed

All processing performed by the Extension is executed **locally** within your browser.

* No data is uploaded to the internet.
* No data is transmitted to external servers.
* No analytics, tracking scripts, advertisements, or remote logging are used.

## Data Storage

The Extension may store configuration data or cached content using Chrome’s local storage APIs. This storage:

* Remains on your device.
* Is never shared externally.
* Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing browser data.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use:

* External APIs
* Cloud storage
* Remote databases
* Third-party analytics

No third parties receive any information through this Extension.

## Your Choices

You may:

* Remove any stored data at any time.
* Stop all data collection by uninstalling the Extension.
* Review or modify what information the Extension has stored by checking your browser's extension data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be included with the Extension before it is published.

## Contact

If you have questions or concerns, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
